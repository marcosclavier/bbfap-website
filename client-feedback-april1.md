## Summary

Frédéric reviewed the V1 of the redesigned site. His overall impression: the site looks too generic / "cookie cutter" and feels cold. He wants it to feel more **personalized** and **warm**, reflecting that BBFAP sells **personal relationships**, not just financial products.

---

## Requested Changes

### 1. More Professional Tone
- The overall look should feel more polished and professional.

### 2. Broaden Client Imagery — More Inclusive
- Current site skews toward a narrow clientele (e.g., dentists only).
- Frédéric also serves the **family market** ("marché familial") and a wider range of professionals.
- Imagery and language should reflect this broader, more inclusive client base.
- Arthur noted they can add more client-type cards/cases as needed.

### 3. Add a Map to the Contact Page
- Frédéric noticed there was no map on the site.
- Arthur agreed to add a map to the **Contact page** so clients can locate the office easily.
- Frédéric wants to "be found easily" ("être repéré facilement").

### 4. Replace Generic Images with Real Photos
- Frédéric will send real photos to replace AI-generated / stock-looking images.
- Goal: make the site feel **less cold and less generic** — more like the actual business.
- Arthur acknowledged the current version looks a bit "cookie cutter" and the real photos will help personalize it.

### 5. YouTube Video Thumbnails (Vignettes)
- Arthur showed a sample custom thumbnail he designed for Frédéric's YouTube videos.
- Plan: create **branded thumbnails for all 9 videos** in the blog/video grid.
- Frédéric to review the sample and provide feedback before applying the style across all videos.

### 6. Blog Section
- The blog page should be built out to properly showcase the YouTube video content.
- Arthur will finalize the blog section layout.

### 7. Emphasize Personal Relationship
- Frédéric stressed that what they sell is the **personal relationship** with clients ("relation personnelle qu'on vend").
- The site's text-heavy SEO approach is understood, but the human/relational side must not be lost.
- Tone should balance SEO content with warmth and approachability.
